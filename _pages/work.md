* 2019 – 2024: Founder and Executive Director, [Florida Justice Center](https://www.fljc.org), Fort Lauderdale, FL
  * Founded and led a 501(c)(3) legal aid nonprofit providing free and sliding-scale services addressing collateral consequences of arrest or conviction including sealing and expungement of arrest records, internet mugshot removal, early termination of probation, driver license reinstatement, and all forms of clemency (rights restoration)
  * Oversaw a staff of twenty paid and volunteer employees serving over 12,000 clients
  * Secured over $1,100,000 in grant funding and donor support
  * Formed coalitions and partnerships, organized one-stop community legal and reentry clinics, and redesigned intake processes to cut routine legal task time by over 75%
  * Successfully advocated for automatic criminal record sealing statute and navigated [The Florida Bar](https://www.floridabar.org/) and [Florida Supreme Court](https://supremecourt.flcourts.gov/) committees to have the law adopted as a new rule of criminal procedure
